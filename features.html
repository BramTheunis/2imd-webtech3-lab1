Discover the features of our COVID-bot

Our bot will help you in all your medical tasks regarding to potential Corona-virus patients. He'll make your tasks easier and your work go faster with more reliability on a day-to-day basis, all the while in stressful environments. Here's how he's planning to do that:

  • Temperature checker for any fever
  • Checking for breathwork through a breathing tube
  • Scanner for any physical anormalities
  • Taking into account previous travels of the patient
  • Checking the blood pressure
  • Calculating the results with the potential incubation period

All of this will only take 30 seconds of time while your patient gets checked by our bot and supervised by you and your colleagues.
